Accommodation

Limited accommodation is available at the Indian Agricultural Research Institute Guest Houses, NASC Guest House for Indian delegates. The above guest houses are close to the congress center. However, a shuttle service can be made available upon request. The delegates interested in accommodation are requested to intimate the organisers preferably by 31st October 2022 as limited accommodation is available. However, the delegates are requested to book their accommodation directly with the Event manager at the Conference website. A number of hotel rooms will be booked at special group rates, including breakfast and VAT. You can reserve your hotel room through the online registration form. The rates will be only valid for reservations made through Event manager. Any requests for hotel accommodation must be accompanied by a credit card number, in order to secure the room. This credit card will not be charged by the organisers but only serves as a reservation guarantee. Participants will have to pay their hotel room and personal expenses at the reception of the hotel. In case of late cancellation or no show, the room will be charged on the credit card.
